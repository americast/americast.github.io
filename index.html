Sayan Sinha

Final year undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur.

I am a fourth-year student at IIT Kharagpur, India.

I like researching on various domains of artificial intelligence. I am also an open source enthusiast.

I was a summer 2019 research intern at Georgia Tech, where I worked on computer vision at the Embedded Pervasive Lab and the Computational Perception Lab. As a part of the Kharagpur RoboSoccer Students' Group (RoboCup team "KgpKubs"), I have worked on path planning, controls and computer vision. Under Google Summer of Code 2018, I have worked on the implementation and optimisation of GPU kernels. Along with that, I was the executive head of Kharagpur Open Source Society in 2018.
